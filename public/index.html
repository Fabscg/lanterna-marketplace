Lanterna Grab n' Go


At Lanterna Grab and Go, we believe great food shouldn't slow you down. Whether you are rushing to work, or just need a quick bite, enjoy authentic Italian cuisine.


1. Call in your order.


2. Let us know the time of pick up.


3. Pay over the phone or at the time of your pick up. You may also place your order and wait. Why not enjoy a draft beer while waiting for your meal? Happy hour all day at $4.99. So what are you waiting for? Grab your favourite dish and get on with your day. Buon Apetito
